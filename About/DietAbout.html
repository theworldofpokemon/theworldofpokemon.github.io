About - Diet

Diet is relatively simple; it entails what the Pokémon eats in the wild. When the Pokémon specifically preys upon certain species, its prey are specifically named as well. Predators that feed on a range of prey instead have the general category that they prey upon listed (ie: "mammals," "fish," etc.). Diet may also include how the Pokémon obtains food if the method is notable.

Note that diet specifically refers to what the Pokémon eats in the wild. Though it usually corresponds to what it eats under a trainer, some Pokémon can be raised on other diets as well. The "Care" section usually details such information.
