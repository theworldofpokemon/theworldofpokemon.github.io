Guide

This quiz is intended to select an ideal starter Pokémon in only five (or fewer) questions. Many times, you will not need all five questions, as some categories are already more narrow than others.


The first question is of habitat; you might want to select the habitat you currently live in, or the habitat you believe you will spend a lot of time exploring; it depends on how you plan to use the results of the quiz.


The second question regards Pokémon maintenance and how much effort you are willing to put towards training your starter. Low-maintenance Pokémon are ideal for beginning trainers, while high-maintenance Pokémon might be more suitable for veterans. Moderate Pokémon are a good choice for a wide variety of trainers.


The third question is of disposition. Would you prefer a reserved, introverted sort of partner, or an outgoing, extroverted one? You might either choose a disposition to match your own, or perhaps one to balance your own tendencies out.


The fourth question pertains to personality. Again, you can either select a personality similar to your own, or a personality that you would want in a partner Pokémon.


The fifth question regards a defining characteristic that you consider most important in a partner. It is usually an ideal or trait that you would want your starter to embody.
